About Us

MCAC 2017 Arsenijević is a reputable company based in Čačak, Serbia, specializing in precision metal machining. Our team has over ten years of experience in the industry and is committed to quality, precision, and innovation. We use modern CNC machines, laser cutting systems, and advanced equipment for grinding, turning, and EDM processing to meet the diverse needs of our clients with high accuracy and efficiency.

We offer a wide range of machining services, including:

  • Milling – High-precision surface machining and complex geometries on various materials.
  • Turning – Precision machining of rotational parts with excellent surface finish.
  • Surface Grinding – Achieving extreme flatness and smoothness on flat surfaces.
  • Cylindrical Grinding – High-precision machining of curved and external surfaces.
  • Wire EDM – CNC contour cutting in hard materials using wire erosion technology.
  • Electrode EDM – Fine detail and cavity shaping using graphite or copper electrodes.
  • Laser Sheet Cutting – Fast and precise cutting of sheet metal using advanced laser technology.

Our mission is to deliver professional and reliable metal processing services, whether for standard components or complex prototypes. We work with clients across various industries, providing flexible, custom-made solutions that meet the highest standards.
